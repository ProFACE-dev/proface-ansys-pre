SPDX-Notes: This license describes the permitted use of the European Union emblem (“EU flag”)
in accordance with Council Regulation (EU) 2022/720 and the European Commission’s
“Rules for the use of the European Union emblem in the context of EU funding”.

Summary:
  - There are no copyright restrictions on the reproduction of the European Union emblem.
  - Use of the emblem by third parties is permitted only if such use is not likely
    to create the impression that there is a connection between the user and
    the European Union or the European Commission, or that the Union or the
    Commission endorses the user or its products or services.
  - Any use of the emblem which the European Commission considers to be inappropriate
    or incompatible with these rules may be prohibited.
  - The emblem must be reproduced in accordance with the graphic standards and
    the rules of use published by the European Commission.

Full legal text:
  Council Regulation (EU) 2022/720 of 26 April 2022 on the use of the European emblem
  by third parties (OJ L 133, 3.5.2022, p. 1–3).
